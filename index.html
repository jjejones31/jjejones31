About me!

Graduating in June 2023, I double majored in Computer Science with a Machine Learning Specialization and Economics with a Data Science Specialization at the University of Chicago. I took a wide variety of classes, from General Chemistry to Social Sciences to Math, Economics and Computer Science. A big theme throughout my life has been challenging myself to learn more and grow to new heights. I still have a plethora of interests and am open to working in any industry.

After spending my previous two summers at Alpine Capital Advisors, I applied my economics and computer science knowledge at Jane Street to work on challenging problems in the trading industry. I developed my skills in data analysis, programming, and communication by working with a team of talented and diverse professionals. I also gained valuable insights into the culture and values of Jane Street, which aligns with my own passion for excellence, innovation, and collaboration.
